HTML Blog FAQ

A blog is used to record or share something with people who visit your site. So the quality comes first, then the pretty css. As I just entered my "Console Month", emacs + HTML suit my demands best. It is quite straightforward and the HTML mode in emacs will make writing HTML easier.

However, when you want to typeset some math equations, you need to do a little extra. You can use \(\KaTeX{}\) or something similar to provide high-quality math via Javascript. Go to katex.org to learn more.
